# 3-Minute Video Script

Target length: 2:45 to 2:55  
Tone: urgent, grounded, human, technically credible  
Video title: "Sahaayak Field: Local AI When The Network Fails"

## 0:00-0:20 - Cold Open

Visual: shaky phone footage style shot of rain, a blocked road, or the sample damaged bridge image.  
Voiceover:

"After a flood, the first report often comes from someone with no command center, no stable internet, and no time. A volunteer sees a damaged bridge, two families cut off, and a phone that barely connects."

On screen: "No internet. One phone. Two minutes."

## 0:20-0:40 - Product Reveal

Visual: open Sahaayak Field. Show the incident intake panel.  
Voiceover:

"Sahaayak Field is a local-first disaster triage assistant powered by Gemma 4. It turns field photos and rough notes into a structured response packet that humans can trust."

## 0:40-1:25 - Live Demo

Visual: upload or load sample image. Show location and volunteer report. Click "Analyze with Gemma 4."  
Voiceover:

"The volunteer adds a scene image, a location hint, and a short report. Gemma 4 analyzes the image and text locally through the model adapter, then returns incident type, severity, next actions, resource needs, and a radio-ready message."

Cut to output:

"Here it marks the case high severity, identifies flood and structural risk, requests responders, first aid, visibility tape, lights, and flotation support, and asks for missing GPS and headcount."

## 1:25-1:55 - Trust Moment

Visual: scroll Evidence Trail and Structured JSON.  
Voiceover:

"The important part is not just the answer. It separates observed facts from inferred risks. It keeps audit notes. It returns JSON that a command center, map, or dispatch tool can consume."

On screen: highlight "Observed facts", "Inference", "Audit".

## 1:55-2:20 - Offline Workflow

Visual: click "Save packet" and show Offline Queue.  
Voiceover:

"If the network is down, packets stay on device. The volunteer can keep working, then sync when signal returns. This is designed for the moment when cloud-only tools fail."

## 2:20-2:40 - Technical Proof

Visual: quick architecture diagram or repo view: browser, Node adapter, Gemma 4/Ollama, prompt file.  
Voiceover:

"The implementation uses a dependency-light browser app, a Node inference adapter, and Gemma 4 through a local Ollama endpoint. Gemma 4 handles multimodal understanding, structured JSON generation, multilingual response, and local reasoning."

## 2:40-2:55 - Closing

Visual: back to field packet and radio message.  
Voiceover:

"Sahaayak Field does not replace responders. It gives them a clearer first packet when minutes matter and the network is gone."

On screen: "Local intelligence for community resilience."

## Required Recording Checklist

- Keep final video under 3 minutes.
- Show the app running, not static slides.
- Show model status in frame.
- Show the repo briefly.
- Show the offline queue.
- Upload to YouTube as public or unlisted.
- Confirm judges can view without login.
